Observations from the plots.

Tumor Response to Treatment Graph
The tumors volume for the mice taking Capomulin shrunk in course of trial, 
while the tumors volume for the mice taking the other drugs grew almost with the same rate.
Thus,the drug Capomulin had a better result.

Metastatic Spread During Treatment Graph
This shows that all tumors had metastatic spread regardless of the drug with Capomulin having the slowest metastatic spread and Placebo having the fastest spread.

Survival During Treatment Graph
This shows that over 80% of the mice taking Capomulin survived during the course of trial while survival rate dropped with almost same rate till day 30 for the other drugs.
After this, the survival rate for Infubinol dropped significantly.

In summary,from the Tumor Change Over 45 Days Treatment graph,Capomulin had most favorable result. This drug could be a great anti cancer drug.
